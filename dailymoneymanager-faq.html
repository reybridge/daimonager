FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do i change the currency?

A: To change the main currency, you must first create an account with the currency you need. To create an account, go to 'Management -> Manage Account' on the side menu. Create a new account by tapping on '+' icon at the right-top of the screen. As you have an account with your desired currency, you can now change your main currency in the preference screen.
