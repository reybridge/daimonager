FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What does 'Currency' setting in preference screen do?

A: You can have more than one account with different currencies. If you enable account management setting in 'Manage Account' page, there will be a new menu on the action bar at the main dashboard to decides the focus you want to observe. The 'Currency' setting decides the focus you are willing to apply every time you initialize the application.

Q: How do i change the currency?

A: To change the main currency, you must first create an account with the currency you need. Then, go to preference screen and tap on 'Currency'. You can now select your currency as the main currency.

Q: How do i create an account?

A: To create an account, go to 'Management -> Manage Account' on the side menu. Create a new account by tapping on '+' icon at the right-top of the screen.

Q: What does the first indicator in analytic page actually do?

A: It shows the probability that you will manage your expenses to stay below the budget you set until the end of the month. The probability is calculated based on your previous transactions in this month. You can simply see it as a grade mark of your daily activities. The higher the value, the more likely you are good in managing money.

Q: I have bought the premium license and i want to switch device. Will my premium license be available on my new device?

A: Yes, your premium license is not bound to the device but the account you used to buy it. You just need to re-buy it anywhere in the application, except you will not be asked to pay. And then close and re-open the app.
