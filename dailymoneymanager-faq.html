FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do i change the currency?

A: To change the main currency, you must first create an account with the currency you need.

Q: How do i create an account?

A: To create an account, go to 'Management -> Manage Account' on the side menu. Create a new account by tapping on '+' icon at the right-top of the screen.

Q: What does the first indicator in analytic page actually do?

A: It shows the probability that you will manage your expenses to stay below the budget you set until the end of the month. The probability is calculated based on your previous transactions in this month. You can simply see it as a grade mark of your daily activities. The higher the value, the more likely you are good in managing money.
